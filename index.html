Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Most websites are completed within 5-7 business days. Simple landing pages can be ready in 3-4 days, while complex full-stack applications may take 2-3 weeks. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage my website?

+

Not at all! We build user-friendly websites and provide simple training on how to make basic updates. For complex changes, we're always available to help. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages if you prefer us to handle everything.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

+

Absolutely! We have a portfolio of websites across various industries including restaurants, salons, retail shops, and professional services. Contact us for a free consultation and we'll show you relevant examples for your industry.

What if I don't like the design?

+

Your satisfaction is our priority! We include 1 rounds of free revisions in all packages. We'll work closely with you during the design phase to ensure the website matches your vision before launch.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

+

Yes! All our websites are 100% mobile-responsive and work perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. With 70%+ users browsing on mobile, we ensure your site looks great on all devices.

Do you provide website hosting and domain?

+

We provide complete deployment services and help you set up hosting and domain. Hosting costs are separate (typically ₹2,000-3,000/year) but we'll guide you through the most affordable and reliable options for your needs.

Can you help with content and images for my website?

+

Yes! We can help with content structure and recommendations. If you provide your business details, we'll organize it professionally. For images, we can use your photos or source professional stock images. Custom photography is available as an add-on service.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

+

Yes! All packages include 7 days of free support after launch. After that, we offer monthly maintenance packages starting from ₹1,500/month which includes updates, backups, security monitoring, and technical support.
